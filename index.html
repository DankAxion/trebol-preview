Our Story

HIALEAH'S
TRUSTED SHOP
SINCE 1994

Trebol Auto Service has been a fixture in the Hialeah community for over 30 years. What started as a commitment to honest, quality automotive care has grown into one of the area's most trusted shops — built one satisfied customer at a time.

Owner Sonny is known throughout the area for taking the time to explain every repair in plain language, offering multiple options to fit your budget, and never doing work without your approval first. That kind of transparency is rare — and it's why so many of our customers have been coming back for decades.

Our shop is clean, our waiting area is comfortable with streaming TV and WiFi, and our team treats every car like it belongs to a family member — because in this community, it usually does.
